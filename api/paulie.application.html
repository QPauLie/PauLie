paulie.application.otoc.average_otoc(generators, v, w)#

Computes the Haar averaged out-of-time-order correlator F between two Pauli strings W and V. We write it as

\[\mathbb{E}_U[F(W, U^\dagger V U)] = \frac{\mathbb{E}_U[\mathrm{tr} (W U^\dagger V U W U^\dagger V U)]}{d}\]

where U sampled according to the Haar measure on the Lie group corresponding to the DLA generated by generators and d is the dimension of the Hilbert space.

We can compute this via the commutator graph as

\[1 - 2 * \frac{|\{W, P\} = 0 : \text{P in connected component of V}|} {|\text{connected component of V}|}\]

(arXiV:2502.16404)

Parameters:

  • generators – Generating set of the Pauli string DLA.

  • v – Pauli string V

  • w – Pauli string W

Returns:

Haar averaged out-of-time-order correlator F between two Pauli strings W and V.
